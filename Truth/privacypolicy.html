Privacy Policy for TrueFighter Android App

Effective Date: 15 may 2024

Thank you for using TrueFighter! This Privacy Policy explains how TrueFighter ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our Android application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide: When you use TrueFighter, we may collect information that you provide directly, including your username, email address, and any content that you submit or upload while using the App.

b. Social Network Information: TrueFighter accesses social network platforms to parse and analyze feeds and user profile data. This may include accessing publicly available information on social media profiles to dismantle false profiles and clean up user profiles.

c. Automatically Collected Information: We may automatically collect certain information about your device, including your IP address, device type, operating system, and mobile network information.

2. How We Use Your Information

a. To Provide and Improve the App: We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve the App, including analyzing how users interact with the App and making improvements based on that analysis.

b. Content Analysis and Profile Cleanup: We use the information collected from social networks to analyze content and profiles to dismantle false profiles and clean up user profiles. This may include automatically blocking users or analyzing content and feeds.

c. Communicate With You: We may use your email address to send you updates about the App, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.

3. How We Share Your Information

a. With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us provide and improve the App. These service providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are only authorized to use your information to the extent necessary to provide services to us.

b. For Legal Purposes: We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or government requests.

4. Your Choices

You can choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of the App.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or by posting the updated Privacy Policy on the App.
