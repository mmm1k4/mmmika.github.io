Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use

Our web app does not collect any personal information unless explicitly provided by the user. We prioritize user privacy and ensure no unauthorized data is gathered.

Google Analytics and Tracking

Our web app may use Google Analytics to track user activity and improve our services. This tracking only occurs if the user explicitly consents to cookies. If cookies are declined, no tracking or analytics data is collected.

Cookies

Our web app may use cookies to enhance user experience. Users can manage their cookie preferences through their browser settings or our provided consent options. Essential cookies may be necessary for basic functionality, while optional cookies (such as analytics and advertising) will only be used with user consent.

Accepting of the cookies will give us permission to use cookie for 30 days or can be revoked by clicking on the cookie and review of the policy.

Log Data

In the event of an error or technical issue, we may collect log data to improve service performance. This data may include information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and timestamps. However, no personally identifiable information is stored unless the user consents to tracking.

Third-Party Services

Our web app may provide connections to third-party services or cloud networks via official APIs. Any data handling by these third parties is subject to their own privacy policies, which users must review and accept before engaging with their services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically for any updates. Any significant changes will be communicated through our web platform.
